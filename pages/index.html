Vice Chancellor's Message

Alumni are the basic constituent and important stakeholders of any higher educational institute. Alumni are life-long family members of the institute and constitute a great force by virtue of their wisdom, innovative minds and ideas, working in the diverse fields at various geographical locations across the world.

As its esteemed alumni, you are also aware that Shri Mata Vaishno Devi Universityis one of the leading institutions in the area of technical, management, humanities, social sciences, languages and literature education. SMVDU has produced many technocrats, entrepreneurs, scientists, role models etc in last many years.

SMVDU, Katra recognizes the potential role that an alumnus can play in fostering its growth and thus initiates an ‘SMVDU ALUMNI ASSOCIATION’, a central body that would commence and coordinate all alumni activities.

I look forward to your valuable thoughts on how you can contribute in this endeavor by sparing some of your valuable time and expertise to the benefit of your Alma Mater and to the community at large.

I once again would like to thank you and hope to receive positive feedbacks very soon.
